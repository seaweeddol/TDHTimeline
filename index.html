Hunter is in a bar, writing an angry letter at the back of an enlistment paper, He sticks the letter to Ms Leading's door with his mother's knife and decide to enlist the army.

Ms Leading saw the departure and throwed away all the letters while going back to the City. A third eye discovers the letters they exchanged, scattered through the beach and reads them

The Poison Woman
Away from the battles, he encounters a lady who takes some pleasure to kill the solders who comes to her. But she sees in Hunter the rest of naivety he still has, and decides to gives him a bottle of poison, maybe if the war is too much for him.

He is awaken from his sleep by a guy, Pierre, who steal dead bodies in researches for ressources. They spend the night talking together.

Saved
Hunter thinks it's the end, his life of suffering is now over, but he's saved by a solder who looks just like him, the Son
Early 1918
He Said He Had A Story
A little time passed since Hunter joined the Son's regiment. A general tells a story about that time when he forced a whore to please him, at the Dime. He can't remember her name, it seems to be a Mistery... (got it?)
Early 1918 - End 1918
This Beautiful Life
Oh, it appears that the General is the Son's Father, and their likeness is not a coincidence. Hunter is destroyed again after discovering in which context he was made, and that his father is an asshole. As time pass, Hunter & the Son are getting closer as Hunter decides to deny his pain
Early 1918 - End 1918
Go Get Your Gun
Time passes as the soldiers fight in the war.
Mid 1918
Son // Father
The Son is dead. In an hopeless act, Hunter decides to steal the Son's identity (because of their likeness) hoping that he can have a better life after the war, with a family and a mother.
To have all chances on his side, he uses the bottle of poison to kill the General while he sleeps.
Mid 1918
Life & Death
The soldiers are singing together in a dramatic way as the war continues, but there's still hope for our new Hunter.
End 1918 - Mid 1919
Rebirth
After fighting in the Second bataille of the Somme, this is the end of WWI (11 Nov 1918). From Storytme: "Act IV takes place in the 1920’s.
End 1918 - Mid 1919
The Old Haunt // Waves
Hunter is in his way back to the City in a military ship, where he speaks with the other passengers about the war. After that, as he contemplates the storm, think about his relation with Ms. Leading, and how it was a sinking. He approaches the city (lighthouse)
Mid 1919 - Mid 1921
At The End Of The Earth
He arrives at the Mother's Home, a little time passes as she live the loss of his husband and accepts her son in spite of some strange behaviour (she maybe blame PTSD)
Mid 1919 - Mid 1921
Remembered
A lot of time passes with the Mother and Hunter is thinking one last time about Ms. Terri. He's saying goodbye to his real mother as he's embrassing the Son's identity
Mid 1921
A Night On The Town // Is There Anybody Here?
The Son's friends kidnap Hunter and bring him to town as he hasn't spoked with them in years. As they wanders between bars, Hunter sees someone familiar, a waiter, a woman... it's Ms Leading, evicting him from the Son's identity and violentely pushing him back into his past. He passes out of inebriation and is wandering in his mind where Hunter's identity went all this time.
Mid - End 1921
The Squeaky Wheel // The Bitter Suite IV & V
Hunter is found by the Son's Fiancée. A little time passes during the song as they get to know each other and she discovers that he is not the Son. She decides after that they should live that lie, and that it's time to get a good life and go to the Church. yes, THE Church. Oh, who's giving sick speeches exchanging salvation for money... yeah, TPP.
End 1921 - End 1922
The Bitter Suite VI
TPP recognize Hunter, but keeps it for him. He tries to persuade Hunter to run for mayor as they speak in the confessionnal, so he can have the City's son under his thumb. A little time passes as Hunter is thinking about this proposition (not knowing that TPP discovered his dirty secret)
1923
King Of Swords (Reversed)
The campaign starts and Hunter is getting popular... and a little over his head.
1923 - 1924
If All Goes Well
After a year of debate and compete, he is elected. The people of the city have big faith in him, and he finally gets of his pedestal
1923 - 1924
The Line
Just after being elected, Hunter decide it's time to separate completely from his old identity. Time passes
1923 - 1924
Wait
As he reflects on his old actions, he give Ms. Leading a redemption, because he realise what he did to her was worse than what she did. They get together again.
End 1924
Ouroboros
After struggling with all the obligations of a mayor, he's ready to take TPP down. But TPP was planning all this, and he presents himself to Hunter, threatening him to expose his identity if he don't cooperate.
1929
Regress
From Storytime: "Act V takes place during the 1930’s" In the meantime, Hunter gets addicted to opium, the Fiancée is now the Wife and they have a Child
1929
The Moon / Awake
Hunter has an hallucination, involving his younger self. He apologize for everything he had become, and is determinated to end this story in a good way. Then he wakes up
End 1929 - Mid 1930
Cascade
Hunter is tore apart between his addiction, his relations with Wife/MsL the people of the City and TPP as they meet each other in the confessionnal
Mid 1930
The Most Cursed Of Hands / Who Am I
TPP is telling a story to the congregation, secretly adressing it to Hunter, telling the story of a gambler who was fool enough to gamble with the Devil.
Mid 1930
The Revival
Oh surprise, the Dime reopens after 5 years of construction and renovation using the mayor's money (but not too much at the same time "it took a little longer than we hoped")
Mid 1930 - End 1932
Melpomene
Time passes, the Dime is full and he spends his time between his private and public life. He writes an ode to Ms. Leading.
Early 1933
Mr. Usher (On His Way To Town)
Mr. Usher, the aptly named and powerfuly evil new character, heard of the new Dime's succes and is on his way to town
Early 1933
The Haves Have Naught
Soon after, Mr. Usher decide to talk with the Mayor. They're arguing about their points of view about the people of the City.
Mid 1933
Light
A little time passes as Hunter takes the Wife and Child to the Lake to protect them from M. Usher
Early 1934
Gloria
As he gets back to the city, he seeks power and confidence to face TPP/Usher in opium. Time passes as M. Usher is waiting for Hunter to gain enough confidence to destabilize TPP.
Mid 1934
The Flame (Is Gone)
Mr Usher now persuades TPP to kill Ms. Leading, so Hunter can be manipulable again. But Mr Usher knows this will only bring chaos.
Mid 1934
The Fire (Remains)
Hunter discovers the body of Ms Leading and burns down the Dime with her inside. The fire spreads to the Church
Mid 1934
The March
TPP, in his Priest role, appeal the people of the city by revealing Hunter's true identity, and saying that Hunter manipulated him (false) and then burned down the House of God (true)
Mid 1934
Blood
Hunter is trapped in his home, the people are waiting outsite. TPP enters the house to face Hunter. Hunter kills TPP
Mid 1934
A Beginning
